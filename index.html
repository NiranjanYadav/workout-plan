General Guidelines

  • Why Push/Pull? Each muscle group is trained twice per week (Mon + Thu for push muscles, Tue + Sat for pull muscles) — optimal frequency for muscle growth.
  • Warm up 5-10 minutes before each session (light cardio + dynamic stretches).
  • Progressive overload: Increase weight by 2-5% when you complete all sets at the top of the rep range.
  • Rest between sets: Compound lifts (squat, deadlift, bench) = 90s-3min. Isolation = 45-60s.
  • Tempo: 2 seconds down, 1 second pause, 1 second up (controlled movements).
  • Breathing: Exhale on exertion (the hard part), inhale on the eccentric (lowering).
  • Hydration: Drink 500ml-1L water per session.
  • Rest days (Wed, Fri, Sun) are full rest. Light walking, stretching, or foam rolling only.
  • Track your weights in the log tables to monitor progress week over week.
  • Badminton (weekends): Can replace Saturday session or be done on a rest day — counts as active recovery.
